Short description:

A customisable app to guide you through HIIT sessions with exercises and timers


Full description:

A High Intensity Interval Training companion app that runs your timers and shows you animated exercises.

This app allows you to:
Manage multiple profiles.
Launch a session including one or more profiles, choosing the types of exercises you want to include or not.
Set up your session with custom rest, work and total durations.
See each profile's own practice statistics.
